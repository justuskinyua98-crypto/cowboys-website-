ARTICLE 5: PROFIT & DIVIDEND POLICY
After payment of operational expenses, taxes, and reinvestment allocations, remaining profits may
be distributed as dividends proportionally according to shareholding (20% each founder).
ARTICLE 6: SHARE TRANSFER RESTRICTIONS
No shareholder may sell, transfer, or pledge shares without unanimous approval of the other
shareholders. In the event of exit, shares must first be offered internally before external sale.
ARTICLE 7: FINANCIAL DISCIPLINE
All company funds shall be managed through an official corporate bank account. Personal
withdrawals without Board approval are strictly prohibited. Proper accounting records and annual
financial reporting are mandatory.
