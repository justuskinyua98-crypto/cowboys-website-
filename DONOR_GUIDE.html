Foundation Donor Guide

How to donate quickly and safely on the Cowboys Group Holdings website.

Back to Website

Step-by-Step (For Donors)

  1. Open the website and go to the Foundation section.
  2. Fill the Foundation Donation form with your details and amount.
  3. Click Submit Donation Intent.
  4. Use the displayed payment details (Till/Paybill/Bank) to send money.
  5. Keep your M-Pesa/Bank transaction reference code.
  6. Share the reference code with foundation support for confirmation.

What Happens Next

  • Your donation intent is logged securely in the system.
  • Admin confirms your payment reference.
  • Record is marked as confirmed.
Tip: use your correct phone/email so support can follow up if needed.

Safety Notes

  • Only send to payment details shown on official website.
  • Do not share OTP/PIN with anyone.
  • Keep transaction confirmation message until acknowledged.
If details are missing, contact the foundation team before sending money.
